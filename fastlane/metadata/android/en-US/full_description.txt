Home Medkit application

Application allows you to easily and quickly register and store information about medicines.

<b>Features</b>

* Automatic addition of medication by scanning the label (only for RU users).
* Adding medication manually.
* Editing information about medicines added manually.
* Sorting of medicines by expiration dates.
* Sound notification of the need to take medicine.
* Selection the main page of the application.

Application will help to avoid forgetting about taking medicines and provide more accurate accounting of them.
